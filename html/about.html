About Me

You might wonder what inspired this website. In short, it comes down to this: I am fascinated by the power of words—that sounds made with our mouths somehow carry meaning and emotion, that marks on a piece of paper can create a story in someone’s mind. Isn’t it incredible that I can type these symbols here at my kitchen counter, and weeks later your brain will be translating them into a coherent message? These words will transcend time and space, connecting my thoughts to yours. It’s amazing.

I grew up in Cedar Hills, Utah, and am currently a student at Brigham Young University hoping to study information systems. However, my love of words continues to shape the way I experience the world, and when I have the time to read or write I feel more connected and grounded.

Here on this site I have included a few small writings of mine. They aren't anything fancy, just some free-verse poetry and scattered thoughts. Most of them weren't written for an audience. But I hope you enjoy reading through them and getting a small sneak-peek into my mind.
